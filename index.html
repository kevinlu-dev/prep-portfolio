ABOUT ME

I'm Kevin, aspiring developer based in Arizona with a background in design. I always had an interest in both the arts and tech. As mentioned earlier I took it upon me to go into the design as a fusion of the two things I was most interested in. Though now with experience behind my back I know it's not what I want to pursue as a life long career. There is no need to try to forcibly combine both tech and the arts, and that became much more clear as I always had the regret in the back of my mind on not jumping after coding earlier, for fear that I'm already too deep into my degree. Well here I am loving programming and illustrating on the side instead. In the end the answer was to keep each to their own and walk each path.
